Role: CloudOps Engineer / SRE

About the role:
Our platform team manages multi-cloud infrastructure serving millions of requests per day. We need an engineer who can automate operations, improve reliability, and build internal tools.

Must-have requirements:
- 3+ years of experience in CloudOps, DevOps, or SRE roles
- Strong experience with Kubernetes and container orchestration in production
- Proficiency with infrastructure-as-code (Terraform, Pulumi, or similar)
- Experience with observability stacks (Prometheus, Grafana, OpenTelemetry)
- Understanding of CI/CD pipelines and GitOps practices
- Scripting and automation skills (Python, Go, or Bash)

Nice to have:
- Multi-cloud experience (AWS, GCP, Azure)
- Experience with service mesh (Istio, Linkerd)
- Familiarity with cost optimization and FinOps practices
- Knowledge of incident management and postmortem culture

What you'll do:
- Design and maintain multi-cloud infrastructure with high availability
- Build internal platform tools to improve developer productivity
- Implement observability, alerting, and incident response workflows
- Drive reliability improvements through SLO-based engineering practices
- Automate manual operational tasks and toil reduction
