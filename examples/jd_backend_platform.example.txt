Role: Backend / Platform Engineer

About the role:
Build and scale the core platform services that power our product. You will design APIs, optimize data layers, and improve developer experience across engineering teams.

Must-have requirements:
- 4+ years of backend engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in one or more of: Python, Go, Rust, Java
- Experience designing RESTful or gRPC APIs at scale
- Solid understanding of relational databases, schema design, and query optimization
- Experience with message queues (Kafka, RabbitMQ, or similar)
- Familiarity with Docker and Kubernetes

Nice to have:
- Experience with microservice architecture and distributed systems patterns
- Familiarity with GraphQL or event-driven architectures
- Contributions to internal platform tools or developer productivity
- Knowledge of authentication and authorization frameworks (OAuth, RBAC)

What you'll do:
- Design and implement core platform APIs and services
- Optimize database performance and data access patterns
- Build internal tools and libraries used by 50+ engineers
- Participate in architecture reviews and design RFCs
- Mentor junior engineers and improve engineering practices
